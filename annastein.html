Anna Steinbauer

Anna is an illustrator and concept artist working with various international clients and publishers such as Wizards of the Coast, Games Workshop, Ulisses Spiele, Simon&Schuster and more.

Her free time is devoted her own passion project Veritas, an illustrated storybook set in a whimsical world of witches, magic and mystery. You can get early access to the art, as well as insight into Anna’s creative process by becoming a patron on Patreon.
