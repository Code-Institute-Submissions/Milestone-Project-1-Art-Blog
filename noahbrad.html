Noah Bradley

Noah Bradley is an American artist primarily known for his work on Magic: The Gathering cards and his The Sin of Man project. He is well known amongst the Reddit community from his frequent contributions to various subreddits.

Up until he was 18, he wanted to be a programmer or video game developer, with just some art on the side, but "art was gonna be the most difficult, eternally challenging thing that I could find to do, and so I went ahead and went for that."
